Who We Are

Construct Direct Co is a trusted manpower and workforce solution provider in Saudi Arabia. With a foundation rooted in integrity and client commitment, we specialize in sourcing, deploying, and managing skilled and semi-skilled workers across multiple sectors. From construction to cleaning, logistics to hospitality, our mission is to deliver manpower that not only meets your expectations but exceeds industry standards. Our strength lies in understanding client needs, maintaining transparency, and executing workforce plans with efficiency and professionalism. Every member of our team is trained, vetted, and guided by a deep commitment to serve both companies and communities.
